Product Analysis

Top 10 Products by Revenue

Jul 2024–Mar 2026 · Revenue from line_item.price × line_item.quantity · oliovita_life_cycle_with_line_items

Volume vs Value Split

3-unit packs drive the most orders (1,274 & 1,006) — entry/trial SKU. 12-unit and Bidón packs drive the most revenue — loyalty SKU. The path from 3-pack buyer → 12-pack/Bidón buyer is the best upsell journey.

Bidón (5L bulk) = Highest CLV Signal

Customers who buy Bidón have avg order value $110K–184K ARS vs $37K for 3-packs. Identify 3-pack repeat buyers and nudge them toward Bidón.
